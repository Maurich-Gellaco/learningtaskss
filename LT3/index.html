Binhi is a song by Arthur Nery, one of my favorite artists in the Philippines. If you listen to the lyrics, you'll notice that this song has a broader definition.

Top 5

Pagsamo

"Pagsamo" is also sung by Arthur Nery. It was released on October 1, 2021 by Viva Records.This song is filled with cherry melodies and raw beats, and it expresses his inner spirit and enthusiasm, making it a nice place to listen.

Top 6

Urong sulong sung by Kiyo and Alisson Shore, this was released in 2017. I used to love this song because of the message. When I hear this song, I always remember my brother singing this song with a cracked voice.

Top 7

So Sick

"So Sick" is a song by Ne-Yo, an American singer-songwriter. It was produced by Eriksen and Hermansen under the production label Stargate. On November 21, 2005, the song was released as the album's second single.

Top 8

Rise

Rise is a song by Jonas Blue, a British DJ and music producer, featuring American pop-rap duo Jack & Jack.Positiva Records and Virgin EMI Records published it on May 25, 2018. I love this song since it is so lively and meaningful.

Top 9

Mama

"Mama" is my top 9 favorite song by Jonas Blue. 'Mama' is a song about that moment of your life when you're young and carefree, and all you care about is having a good time with your friends every day and night," Blue stated of the song.

Top 10

Sign of the Times

"Sign of the Times" is Harry Styles' debut song off his self-titled debut studio album. It produced by Bhasker, Salibian, and Johnson. It was released on April 7, 2017 by Columbia Records.
