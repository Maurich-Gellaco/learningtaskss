WHO WE ARE

We are a team that encourages everyone to protect the environment. This is for our future and to the next generation. The team is asking for a help by simply being responsible to everything that we do, do an action, and take care of the environment. Everything we do is always have a consequences, so we should be mindful for our own good. Furthermore, the main goal of the team is to open the hearts of everyone to do an action in the places/areas most affected by pollution.
