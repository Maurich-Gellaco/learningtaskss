Vegetarian Rice

Ingredients

  • 1/2 chopped onion

  • 1 chopped garli clove

  • 1 chopped courgette

  • 1 grated carrot

  • 1 chopped red and yellow pepper

  • 75g frozen peas

  • 1/2 tin of chopped tomatoes

  • 350ml Vegetable stock

  • 150g Dried brown rice

  • 1 tbsp of dried parsley

  • 1 tsp tumeric

  • Seasoning

Method

  1. Serves 2: Prep time 10 mins: cook time 25 mins

  2. Add onion, garlic and tumeric to pan with a splash of veg stock

  3. Stirring, cook gently for a 3 mins then add the chopped courgette, grated carrot and chopped pepper

  4. Stir well for a further 3 mins then add the dried rice

  5. Stir rice in well with the vegetables

  6. Once rice is distributed well throughout the mixture add the stock- leave to boil

  7. once boiled immediately turn heat down to simmer and cover.Cook for 10 mins

  8. Add in chopped tomatoes and frozen peas and stir

  9. Cover and simmer for a further 1o mins

  10. Once cooked stir in dried parsely with seasoning

  11. Spoon rice into 2 dishes
